The paper "Health Condition Estimation for Discrete Power Electronic Devices under Package Failure," with Professor Zhonghai Lu as the first author, received the 2024 IEEE Reliability Society Best Conference Paper Award. The award was presented at IEEE/ASQ RAMS-EUROPE in Trondheim, Norway, in June 2026.
